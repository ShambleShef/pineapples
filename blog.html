The Pineapples

This blog is all about the use and misuse of pineapples.

The right way to use a pineapple

The proper way to use a pineapple is:

  • On a pizza.
  • When visiting Hawaii
  • On a roast ham

The wrong way to use a pineapple

Pineapples are not meant for:

  • On Instagram
  • For decoration
  • As a weapon

About Me
